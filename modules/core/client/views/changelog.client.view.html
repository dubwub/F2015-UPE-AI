12/3/16

BALANCE PATCH: Starting on turn 400, the apocalypse will occur. An explosion trail will spawn in the top right and in the bottom left. The bottom left trail will initially move up, moving alongside the edge of the board with each complete turn (moving once after both players have moved). The top right trail will initially move down. Both trails will spiral inwards, turning 90 degrees when colliding either with the end of the board or with the other trail. Note that there is a small bug with this in practice mode, as the trails seem to move twice in a row. When testing for the tiebreaker make sure to use the matchmaking server.
